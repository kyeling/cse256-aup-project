Fraudulent or illegal use of Zoom Contact Center Services, including any activity that violates telemarketing laws or regulations.
b. Auto-dialing; trunking; traffic pumping. Perform auto-dialing or “predictive dialing” in an abusive manner; trunk or forward your Zoom number to other numbers that handle multiple simultaneous calls or to a private branch exchange (PBX) or a key system; traffic pumping or access stimulation of calls through the Services or the Zoom Network.
